Most AI projects fail due to overpromising and unrealistic expectations. This article outlines Nomis's pragmatic approach to AI implementation, focusing on practical applications that create measurable value.

**Key Topics:**
- Why most AI projects fail and how to avoid common pitfalls
- Three core AI use cases at Nomis: data analysis, recommendation engines, and decision support
- The reality of AI implementation: augmenting rather than replacing human judgment
- Accelerating deposit pricing analysis across massive datasets
- Building AI business analysts that provide nuanced recommendations
- Creating decision support systems that explain trade-offs
- Measuring ROI and demonstrating tangible business value
