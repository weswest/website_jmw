# Image Generation Prompts

Tracking prompts used to generate hero images for articles.

## multi-agent-microservices-hero.jpg
**Article:** It's Just Microservices and Team Management
**Generator:** Grok
**Prompt:** "Split screen illustration: left side shows a microservices architecture diagram with connected boxes, right side shows an org chart with the same structure, connected by a subtle equals sign or mirror line, minimal flat design, dark background with warm highlights, wide format 1200x630, no text"

## policy-enforcement-hero.jpg
**Article:** Policy Enforcement for Agentic Workflows
**Generator:** Grok
**Prompt:** "Three-layer security architecture visualization: bottom layer shows a document/manual icon (convention), middle layer shows physically separated office rooms with walls between them (isolation), top layer shows a lock/shield enforcing boundaries (structural enforcement). Dark background, warm gold and white accents, clean geometric style, wide format 1200x630, no text"
